Frequently Asked Questions

What is CareerBridge?

CareerBridge is a platform that connects job seekers and interns with employers looking for talent.

How do I create an account?

Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and fill in your details to create an account.

How do I search for jobs or internships?

Use the search bar on the homepage to filter opportunities by location, industry, and other criteria.

Can I track my job applications?

Yes! Our Application Tracker helps you monitor all your applications in one place.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page, click on 'Forgot Password,' and follow the instructions.

Is my personal information safe?

We use advanced encryption to protect your personal data. Your privacy is our priority.

If you have more questions, please contact us.
